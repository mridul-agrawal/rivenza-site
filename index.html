Rivenza, an independent game studio building an original fantasy world rooted in Indian storytelling through a tactical RPG

Rivenza is a one-person game studio from India. It exists because two great traditions have never met: Strategy games have given the world some of its most memorable battles, yet for decades the genre has spoken almost entirely in the language of castles and dragons.

Indian games, meanwhile, have grown remarkably over the last decade, and developers here have proven that world-class experiences can be built from this soil. Within that rise, one space has remained nearly untouched: the tactical RPG genre.

This genre runs on choice, consequence, and the bonds between characters. So do the stories our land has told for thousands of years. Rivenza is building that meeting place: an original fantasy world rooted in Indian storytelling, made for players everywhere.
